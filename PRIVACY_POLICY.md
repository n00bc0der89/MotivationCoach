# Privacy Policy for History Motivation Coach

**Last updated:** January 31, 2026

## Overview

History Motivation Coach ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the App handles data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any information to external servers.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

- **Motivation Content**: Historical quotes, images, and metadata are stored in a local database
- **Delivery History**: Records of which motivations you've received and when
- **User Preferences**: Your notification settings and preferences
- **Seed Data Flag**: A simple flag indicating whether initial content has been loaded

This data never leaves your device and is only accessible by the App.

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

### POST_NOTIFICATIONS
- **Purpose**: Required to send motivational notifications to you
- **Usage**: Used only to display scheduled motivational content
- **Data**: No data is collected or transmitted

### RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
- **Purpose**: Required to reschedule notifications after your device restarts
- **Usage**: Ensures your notification schedule continues after a reboot
- **Data**: No data is collected or transmitted

### SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM
- **Purpose**: Required for precise notification timing
- **Usage**: Ensures notifications arrive at your configured times
- **Data**: No data is collected or transmitted

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics services
- No advertising networks
- No crash reporting services
- No cloud storage services
- No social media integrations

## Data Sharing

**We do not share any data** because we do not collect any data. All information remains on your device.

## Data Retention

Data is retained on your device until you:
- Clear the app's data through Android settings
- Uninstall the App
- Use the "Clear History" feature within the App

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: All data is stored locally and accessible through the App
- **Deletion**: Uninstall the App or clear app data to delete all information
- **Portability**: Data remains on your device and is not locked to any account

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Data security depends on your device's security measures
- We recommend using device encryption and screen locks
- The App does not transmit data over networks
