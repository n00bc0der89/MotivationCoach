## FAQ for Store Listing

**Q: Does this app require an internet connection?**
A: Only for the initial download. After installation, the app works completely offline.

**Q: Will I see repeated quotes?**
A: No! Our smart algorithm ensures you never see the same quote twice until you've seen all content.

**Q: How many quotes are included?**
A: The app launches with 100+ carefully curated historical quotes, with more coming in future updates.

**Q: Does the app collect my data?**
A: No. All data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or share any information.

**Q: Can I choose when I receive notifications?**
A: Yes! You can set a time window for even distribution or pick specific times throughout the day.

**Q: Is there a subscription fee?**
A: No. The app is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
